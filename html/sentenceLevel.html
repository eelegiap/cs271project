Word Frequency

This visualization displays the most frequently used words in each text. We’ve used the tf-idf algorithm instead of raw count to display data so as to provide information that is more useful for translation and literary analysis. Hovering over each bar will display information about how many times the word is used, along with the number of sentences the word has appeared in. The “Elements” selection box allows users to choose whether they would like to see 5, 10, or 15 elements displayed. The “Text” selection box allows users to choose whether they would like to see word frequencies for the source or translation document.

Sentence Length

This visualization displays the distribution of sentences based on their lengths. Sentence length is measured on the x-axis and frequency is measured on the y-axis. Hovering over an individual bar will provide specific information on the sentence length corresponding to the selected bar and the frequency at which sentences of said length occur.
